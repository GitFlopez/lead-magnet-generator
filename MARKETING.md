# Marketing Copy — Lead Magnet & Opt-In Content Generator

## ClawHub Marketplace Listing

**Tagline:** Generate a complete lead magnet funnel in one afternoon — checklist, ebook, quiz, or full landing page + delivery email.

**Description:**

Every email list starts with a lead magnet. Most people never build one because creating the asset, the landing page, and the delivery email all at once is overwhelming. This skill does all three.

Four prompt templates. One for each lead magnet format that converts:

- **Checklist** — the fastest to create, highest opt-in rate for step-by-step topics
- **Ebook / Mini-Guide** — 5-7 chapter outline with intro, chapter promises, and author bio
- **Quiz** — 5 questions, 3 result segments, results pages, email gate copy (2-3x higher opt-in than static)
- **Landing Page + Delivery Email** — A/B headline variants, 3-bullet benefit stack, opt-in form copy, and a delivery email that builds the relationship from the first click

Each prompt is built around conversion principles that actually work — AIDA framing, identity-first result segments, benefit stacks that answer "what will I DO" not "what does it contain," and delivery emails that give immediate value before the reader opens the PDF.

**No prior copywriting experience needed.** Fill in your topic, audience, pain point, and outcome. The prompt does the rest.
