## Overview

Generate high-converting lead magnets that grow your email list on autopilot. This skill produces four types of lead magnets — checklist, ebook outline, quiz, and email opt-in copy — with full landing page and delivery email copy included.

Lead magnets are the top of every sales funnel. Without a compelling reason to opt in, you're losing 95% of your traffic. This skill builds the asset, the opt-in page, and the delivery sequence so you can launch a lead gen funnel in one afternoon.

## Prompts

### Prompt 1: Checklist & Quick-Win Guide
Generate a complete checklist lead magnet with 10-15 actionable items, organized into logical sections, with a short explanation for each item. Includes PDF-ready formatting, a title formula optimized for opt-in rates, and a one-paragraph teaser for the landing page.

### Prompt 2: Ebook / Mini-Guide Outline
Generate a 5-7 chapter ebook or mini-guide outline with chapter titles, 3-5 bullet points per chapter, an introduction hook, and a conclusion with CTA. Includes a title and subtitle pair tested against AIDA formula, plus an "about the author" blurb template.

### Prompt 3: Quiz Lead Magnet
Generate a 5-question scored quiz with 3-4 answer options per question, scoring logic, 3 result segments with personalized recommendations, and a results page for each segment. Includes quiz title formula (high opt-in: "What type of X are you?"), intro copy, and email capture CTA between quiz and results.

### Prompt 4: Opt-In Landing Page + Delivery Email
Generate complete opt-in landing page copy (headline, subheadline, 3-bullet benefit stack, social proof placeholder, CTA button text) plus the delivery email (subject line, body, download link placeholder, P.S. upsell teaser). Includes A/B test variants for headline and CTA.

## Inputs Required

- **Topic / niche** (e.g., "email marketing for coaches", "home organization", "freelance pricing")
- **Target audience** (e.g., "early-stage SaaS founders", "busy moms", "B2B sales reps")
- **Lead magnet type** (checklist / ebook / quiz / landing page)
- **Primary pain point** (what problem does this solve?)
- **Your name / brand** (for delivery email and author bio)
